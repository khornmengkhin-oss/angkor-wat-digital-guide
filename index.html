About this project

One guide, five small pages

Angkor Wat is one of Cambodia's most important places. Our group made this website to help visitors and students understand the temple in a simple way. Each page has one main topic, so the project is easier for our team to work on.

  • Home page gives the first introduction.
  • History page talks about the Khmer Empire and religion.
  • Architecture page explains the temple design.
  • Gallery page shows pictures and captions.
  • Visit page gives simple travel advice.

Quick facts

Small facts about Angkor Wat

  1. Angkor Wat was built in the early 12th century.
  2. It was connected to King Suryavarman II.
  3. The temple was first built for Hindu worship and later became Buddhist.
  4. It is famous for towers, long galleries, and stone carvings.
  5. Angkor became a UNESCO World Heritage Site in 1992.

World heritage

Why it matters

Angkor Wat is not only a temple for tourism. It is also part of Cambodian identity, history, art, religion, and national pride. That is why our group chose it for this website project.
