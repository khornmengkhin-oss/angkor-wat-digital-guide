The Angkor Wat

Angkor Wat is a temple complex in Cambodia and is the world's largest religious monument.

UNESCO World Heritage Site

Angkor Wat is part of the Angkor Archaeological Park, a UNESCO World Heritage Site in Cambodia. Inscribed in 1992, Angkor is recognized for its outstanding Khmer architecture, artistic achievements, and historical importance as the center of the Khmer Empire. UNESCO and Cambodia continue to work together to preserve this cultural treasure for future generations.
